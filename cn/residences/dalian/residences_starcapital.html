CRES&ASIA Group

CRES&ASIA Group, founded in Britain in 1998, is to provide hotel management services, consulting services, agency services and other investment services. Since entering China, we have quickly become the benchmark for comprehensive real estate service company.
As a subsidiary of CRESA Group, CRES&ASIA RESIDENCE GROUP manages and operates many high-end properties and serviced apartments in different cities in China. Its aim is to build Asia's leading brand of high-end serviced apartments and properties.
