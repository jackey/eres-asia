CRES&ASIA Group

CRES&ASIA Group, founded in Britain in 1998, is to provide hotel management services, consulting services, agency services and other investment services. Since entering China, we have quickly become the benchmark for comprehensive real estate service company.
As a subsidiary of CRESA Group, CRES&ASIA RESIDENCE GROUP manages and operates many high-end properties and serviced apartments in different cities in China. Its aim is to build Asia's leading brand of high-end serviced apartments and properties.

CRES & Asia Group provides seamless integrated services covering all project management, hospitality, and marketing , including;

  • Development Consultancy
  • Concept Development
  • Project Management
  • Pre-opening Services
  • Management Operation Services
  • Launch and marketing services
  • On-going asset services

Solutions in the Planning & Consultancy Phase range from a Market Analysis to creating the Concept Book. CRES & Asia Group offers integrated assistance to Architects and Interior Designers and, depending on location of your property, are in a position to recommend an Operator if required. We can also help secure the finances required to develop your dream．

  • Advise on project development from operator’s perspective
  • Advise on business risk analysis
  • Advise on strategic positioning
  • Feasibility study
  • Marketing strategy
  • Financial Advisory Services
